01

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Fieldstream LLC ("Fieldstream," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use the Allout mobile application and related services (the "Service").

Allout is a social platform for the outdoor community that includes profiles, posts, reels, comments, direct messaging, marketplace listings, trophy content, and related features.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this Privacy Policy.

02

Information We Collect

A. Account & Profile Information

  • Name, username, display name, email address, password/authentication information, profile photo, bio, and profile details you choose to provide
  • Account identifiers, user IDs, and authentication records used to operate your account

B. User Content

  • Photos, videos, audio contained in videos, captions, comments, posts, reels, trophy entries, marketplace listings, listing descriptions, pricing, saved content, likes, follows, and other content you submit or display through the Service
  • Direct messages and communications you send to other users through the Service

C. Marketplace-Related Information

  • Listing details you provide, including item descriptions, photos/videos, price, condition, category, general location, and communications with other users
  • We do not process payments, escrow funds, arrange shipping, or store payment card information for user-to-user marketplace activity

D. Location Information

  • General location information you provide, such as city, state, or listing location
  • Approximate location information, including location derived from IP address or device settings
  • Precise location is only collected if you grant permission through your device settings for features that require it

E. Device, Diagnostics & Usage Information

  • Device type, operating system, app version, device settings, log data, crash reports, performance data, and diagnostic information
  • Usage data such as app launches, taps, clicks, scrolling, searches, views, likes, saves, follows, messages, marketplace interactions, and other product interaction data

F. AI Assistant Information

  • Prompts, questions, messages, generated responses, and related interaction data submitted to or generated by the Allout AI assistant
  • This information may be used to provide, maintain, improve, and moderate the AI assistant experience

G. Support & Safety Communications

  • Information you provide when contacting support, reporting users, reporting content, appealing moderation decisions, or sending feedback

  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, spam, abuse, security incidents, illegal activity, and violations of our Terms
